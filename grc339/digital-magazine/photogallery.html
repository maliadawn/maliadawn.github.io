July's Must Have Accessories

There’s something about July that invites bold choices. Maybe it’s the longer days, the spontaneous getaways, or just the freedom that comes with the heart of summer—but this is the time to have fun with fashion. Layers get lighter, colors get louder, and accessories take center stage. Whether you’re dressing for golden hour, a last-minute road trip, or a casual coffee run that turns into a full-day adventure, it’s the details that pull everything together. And this month’s picks are all about embracing contrast, confidence, and just the right amount of chaos.

Chunky sunglasses are topping every look right now—oversized, angular, and unapologetically dramatic. They bring an instant cool factor, turning even the most low-effort outfit into something intentional. Paired with bold jewelry—think sculptural shapes, stacked rings, and eye-catching earrings like delicate seashells—you get that perfectly unpolished summer energy that feels effortless and expressive all at once.

Shoes are getting playful, too. Socks with heels are no longer just a runway moment—they’ve become a bold styling move anyone can pull off. It’s quirky and unexpected in the best way, offering a refreshing break from the usual sandals. Still, fun shoes of all kinds are having a moment, whether it’s color-soaked slides or vintage-inspired sneakers. And don’t underestimate the power of boots in summer—especially when paired with floaty dresses or denim shorts. They add an edge that’s tough to beat.

Texture is everywhere this month, and it’s doing more than just looking pretty. Beaded bags add a handcrafted, slightly nostalgic vibe that feels right at home on rooftops, boardwalks, or packed into your beach tote. They’re playful, eye-catching, and full of personality. And for when the sun dips behind the clouds or the breeze picks up, a quilted jacket is the answer. Lightweight but structured, it’s the perfect balance of function and fashion—especially in soft pastels or bold color blocks that echo summer’s high energy.

These are the pieces that aren’t afraid to make a little noise. This season, let your accessories do the talking—because July is about showing up, standing out, and having a little fun with it all.
