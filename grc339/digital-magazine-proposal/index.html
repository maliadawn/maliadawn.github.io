Introduction

Mosaic

Mosaic is an edgy fashion magazine that captures the pulse of Gen Z and Millenial fashion through the lens of street style, internet-born trends, and the influential celebrities and creators shaping them. The magazine is a combination of fashion, digital culture, and youth expression.
